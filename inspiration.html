Quotes About Life

"It is not how long but how well we live that matters."
— Jeremiah Say

"Life is not a solo but a chorus. We live in relationships from cradle to grave."
— Anon.

"Life is worth living better than most men live it."
— Anon.

"Live your best, and act your best, and think your best each day, for there may be no tomorrows."
— Anon.

"Everything in life is a test of character."
— John Blanchard

"Life ought not merely to contain acts of worship, it should be an act of worship."
— John Blanchard

"Be such a man, and live such a life, that if every man were such as you, and every life a life like yours, this earth would be God’s paradise."
— Phillips Brooks

"The quality of life is more important than life itself."
— Alexis Carrel

"Life is too short to be small."
— Benjamin Disraeli

"The man who regards his own life and that of his fellow creatures as meaningless is not merely unfortunate but almost disqualified for life."

Inspirations on Success

"No one ever climbed the ladder of success with his hands in his pockets."
— Jeremiah Say

"Patience, persistence and perspiration make an unbeatable combination for success."
— Napoleon Hill

"Always expect to succeed, and never think you have succeeded."
— Napoleon Hill

"I'm a success today because I had a friend who believed in me and I didn't have the heart to let him down."
— Abraham Lincoln

"Victory is sweetest when you’ve known defeat."
— Malcolm X

"The secret of success is constancy to purpose."
— Benjamin Disraeli

"The secret of success in life is for a man to be ready for his opportunity when it comes."
— Benjamin Disraeli

"You learn more from failure than from successes. Don’t let it stop you. Failure builds character."
— Anon

"A man is a success if he gets up in the morning and gets to bed at night, and in between he does what he wants to do."
— Bob Hope

"Success does not consist in never making mistakes but in never making the same one a second time."
— George Bernard Shaw

"Try not to become a man of success but rather try to become a man of value."
— Albert Einstein

Quotes on Love

"Love is the master key that opens the gates of happiness."
— Oliver Wendell Holmes

"Love is the only force capable of transforming an enemy into a friend."
— Martin Luther King Jr.

"One word frees us of all the weight and pain of life: That word is love."
— Sophocles

"The loneliest place in the world is the human heart when love is absent."
— Jeremiah Say

"Jealousy, properly considered, is an essential element of true love: it is an unceasing longing for the loved one’s welfare."
— J. A. Motyer

"Love is service rather than sentiment."
— John R. W. Stott

"Love is the outgoing of the entire nature in self-sacrificing service."
— W. H. Griffith Thomas

"You can give without loving, but you cannot love without giving."
— Amy Carmichael

"Love is like a friendship caught on fire. In the beginning a flame, very pretty, often hot and fierce, but still only light and flickering. As love grows older, our hearts mature and our love becomes as coals, deep-burning and unquenchable."
— Bruce Lee

"Love takes off masks that we fear we cannot live without and know we cannot live within."
— James Baldwin
